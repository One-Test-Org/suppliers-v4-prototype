Does your organisation have any connected persons?

A ‘connected person‘ can be any of the following:
  • a person with significant control over the supplier
  • a relevant legal entity,
  • a director or shadow director of the supplier
  • a parent undertaking or a subsidiary undertaking of the supplier
  • an equivalent to any of the above
  • an individual or organisation with the right to exercise, or who actually exercises, significant influence or control over the supplier, or over which the supplier has the right to exercise, or actually exercises, significant influence or control
